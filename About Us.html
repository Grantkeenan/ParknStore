Solving New Zealand’s Hidden Space Problem

Making parking and storage accessible, affordable, and local — while helping homeowners earn extra income from the space they already have.

Our Mission

Across Auckland and other growing cities, homes are getting smaller, driveways are disappearing, and storage is becoming harder to find. Medium‑density housing, townhouses, and apartments often come with limited or no parking, and almost no room for the things people actually own — boats, trailers, tools, or even the extra boxes that come with moving house.

At the same time, thousands of homeowners across the region have unused driveways, empty garages, spare sheds, and open land sitting idle. ParknStore was created to connect these two groups through a safe, managed, and reliable platform that makes renting space simple for everyone.

We aim to provide a trusted marketplace where both sides benefit — and where the platform is actively maintained, supported, and improved to keep the experience smooth and secure.

Why This Matters Right Now

Demand for parking and storage continues to rise while supply shrinks. We bridge the gap.

For Guests

Whether you need:

  • A parking spot close to work
  • Secure place to store your boat
  • Temporary storage while moving
  • Long‑term space for a trailer, caravan or classic car

ParknStore helps you find affordable, nearby options from real people in your community — with the confidence of a platform that handles bookings, payments, and support.

For Hosts

If you have:

  • A driveway
  • A garage or carport
  • A spare shed or basement
  • A patch of unused land

Turn it into passive income by renting it out to people who genuinely need it. We provide the tools, visibility, and support to make hosting simple — and our platform fees help us maintain a safe, reliable service for everyone.
