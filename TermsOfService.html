MVP (Concierge) Service Phase

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

Important Notice: ParknStore is currently in its initial launch phase, operating as a minimal viable product (MVP). We are manually facilitating connections and payment processing between early-adopter Hosts and Guests as we build our full automated platform. Service during this phase is provided on a "best-effort" basis.

Nature of This Phase

During this MVP phase, ParknStore acts as a manual facilitator and payment intermediary. Our team will help connect interested Hosts and Guests, facilitate the creation of rental agreements, and process payments to test our systems. This process is designed to validate our service in a real-world setting with a limited number of users.

Payment Processing During MVP

No service fees will be charged by ParknStore during this MVP phase. We are manually processing payments to test our systems. The full amount paid by the Guest will be disbursed to the Host, minus only any actual payment processing costs we incur from third-party providers. This allows us to validate our platform before introducing our standard service fee of 15% or less at launch.

Limitation of Liability in MVP Phase

Given the manual and testing nature of this phase, ParknStore's liability is further limited. We facilitate connections and payments in good faith but cannot guarantee the performance, suitability, or reliability of Users. All interactions and agreements between Users are at their own risk. Our total liability arising from the MVP service is limited to NZD $100.

Transition to Full Platform

Upon official launch of the automated ParknStore platform, all future bookings and transactions will be governed by our standard Terms of Service (outlined in the sections below), which will include automated payment processing and the applicable service fee. Users will be notified before this transition occurs.

2. Definitions

  • ParknStore: The ParknStore.co.nz Platform, operated by the founder and team, which connects Hosts and Guests for parking and storage solutions.
  • Host: A person or entity listing a parking or storage space on the ParknStore platform.

ParknStore acts as a payment processor and platform facilitator. We are not a party to rental agreements between Hosts and Guests. We provide the technology platform to facilitate connections and process payments but do not own, manage, or insure spaces and items parked/stored at the space.

Platform as Payment Processor: ParknStore processes all payments between Hosts and Guests. We collect payments from Guests and distribute funds to Hosts, minus our service fee.

5. Payment Processing

MVP Phase Notice: During the MVP phase, we are manually processing payments to test our systems. No service fees are being charged, and the full amount paid by Guests (minus any actual payment processing costs we incur) is being disbursed to Hosts.

Service Fees

ParknStore charges a competitive service fee of 15% or less on each booking. This fee is deducted before disbursing funds to Hosts and allows us to provide a secure, reliable platform and continuous customer support. The exact fee will be clearly displayed before confirmation. Guests pay the full advertised price with no additional fees.

Payment Collection

We collect payments from Guests at the time of Booking confirmation. Funds are held securely until 24 hours after the rental period begins, then released to the Host (minus our service fee).

Payout to Hosts

Hosts will receive payment for confirmed Bookings within 3-5 business days after the Booking period begins, minus the ParknStore service fee.

6. Refund Policy

How ParknStore Processes Refunds

ParknStore acts as a payment intermediary. While we strive to pay our Hosts promptly, we understand that situations requiring a refund may arise. Here is our process for handling refunds after funds have been disbursed to the Host.

1. The Refund Agreement is Paramount

All refund requests must first be discussed and mutually agreed upon between the Host and the Guest. ParknStore will not initiate a refund without written confirmation from both parties.

2. The Process: Reversing the Transaction

When a refund is agreed upon after we have paid the Host, the process involves the Host repaying the amount to ParknStore, or ParknStore deducting from future payouts if the Host is uncooperative.

3. Service Fees on Refunds

Our service fee is also refunded, meaning the Host is refunded their full payout, and the Guest receives a full refund.

8. Cancellation Policy

Standard Cancellations

Host-Initiated Cancellations

If a Host cancels a confirmed booking after the grace period, they may be subject to penalties including temporary suspension.
