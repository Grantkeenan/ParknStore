MVP (Concierge) Service Phase

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

Important Notice: ParknStore is currently in its initial launch phase, operating as a minimal viable product (MVP). We are manually facilitating connections and payment processing between early-adopter Hosts and Guests as we build our full automated platform. Service during this phase is provided on a "best-effort" basis.

1. Nature of This Phase

During this MVP phase, ParknStore acts as a manual facilitator and payment intermediary. Our team will help connect interested Hosts and Guests, facilitate the creation of rental agreements, and process payments to test our systems. This process is designed to validate our service in a real-world setting with a limited number of users.

2. Facilitated Process

If a promising connection is found between a Host and Guest:

  • Introduction: ParknStore will facilitate an introduction between the parties.
  • Agreement Assistance: We will provide a rental agreement template and guidance. However, the final agreement is directly between the Host and the Guest ("Users").
  • Payment Facilitation: ParknStore will manually process payments between Users to test our systems and procedures. Guests will pay ParknStore, and ParknStore will disburse funds to Hosts.
  • No Agency: ParknStore is not a party to any rental agreement and does not act as an agent for either User.

3. Payment Processing During MVP

No service fees will be charged by ParknStore during this MVP phase. We are manually processing payments to test our systems. The full amount paid by the Guest will be disbursed to the Host, minus only any actual payment processing costs we incur from third-party providers. This allows us to validate our platform before introducing our standard service fee of 15% or less at launch.

4. Limitation of Liability in MVP Phase

Given the manual and testing nature of this phase, ParknStore's liability is further limited. We facilitate connections and payments in good faith but cannot guarantee the performance, suitability, or reliability of Users. All interactions and agreements between Users are at their own risk. Our total liability arising from the MVP service is limited to NZD $100.

5. Transition to Full Platform

Upon official launch of the automated ParknStore platform, all future bookings and transactions will be governed by our standard Terms of Service (outlined in the sections below), which will include automated payment processing and the applicable service fee. Users will be notified before this transition occurs.

Payout to Hosts

6. Refund Policy

MVP Phase Notice: During the MVP phase, refunds will be handled manually by our team. The process outlined below describes how refunds will be handled once the automated platform launches.

How ParknStore Processes Refunds

ParknStore acts as a payment intermediary. While we strive to pay our Hosts promptly, we understand that situations requiring a refund may arise. Here is our process for handling refunds after funds have been disbursed to the Host.

1. The Refund Agreement is Paramount

All refund requests must first be discussed and mutually agreed upon between the Host and the Guest. ParknStore will not initiate a refund without written confirmation from both parties, typically via our messaging system.

2. The Process: Reversing the Transaction

When a refund is agreed upon after we have paid the Host, the process involves two potential steps:

Step A: Host-Cooperated Refund (Preferred Method)

  • We will contact the Host and request that they repay the refund amount to ParknStore.
  • Once we receive the funds from the Host, we will immediately process the full refund to the Guest's original payment method.
  • This is the fastest and most straightforward method.

Step B: ParknStore-Mediated Refund (If Host is Unresponsive/Uncooperative)

  • If the Host agrees to the refund but does not voluntarily repay the amount, ParknStore will deduct the refund sum from the Host's future payouts.
  • For example: If a Guest is owed a $100 refund, the next $100 earned by the Host from other bookings will be retained by ParknStore and used to fund the Guest's refund.
  • Once the full amount is secured, we will process the refund to the Guest.

3. Service Fees on Refunds

Our service fee, which was deducted from the original transaction, will also be refunded to the Host. This means the Host is refunded their full payout amount, and the Guest is refunded the full amount they paid.

Example: On a $100 booking, ParknStore took a $15 fee and paid the Host $85. For a full refund, the Host must repay $85, and ParknStore returns the $15 fee, allowing us to refund the Guest the full $100.

4. Timing

Refunds are processed to the Guest's original payment method within 5-10 business days from the date the funds are secured from the Host.

The time it takes for the refund to appear in the Guest's account depends on their bank or card issuer's processing times.

5. Important Conditions

  • ParknStore's ability to process a refund is contingent on the Host's cooperation or their future use of our platform.
  • We reserve the right to suspend a Host's account if they refuse to comply with a mutually agreed-upon refund.
  • ParknStore is not liable for refunds if a Host leaves the platform and has no future earnings.

8. Cancellation Policy

Host-Initiated Cancellations

If a Host cancels a confirmed booking after the grace period, they may be subject to penalties including temporary suspension from the platform.
