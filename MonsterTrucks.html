Frequently Asked Questions

What is ParknStore's role in the parking arrangements?

ParknStore acts solely as a connection service for the Monster Trucks Show at Eden Park. We:

  • Connect homeowners with available parking spaces to drivers needing parking
  • Facilitate the initial introduction by sharing contact information
  • Help match based on location and requirements

We are not involved in:

  • Payment processing or financial transactions
  • Space agreements or contracts between parties
  • Dispute resolution between homeowners and drivers
  • Verifying the condition or suitability of parking spaces

All arrangements, payments, and agreements are made directly between homeowners and drivers.

How does the connection process work?

It's simple:

  1. You submit your details through our form
  2. We match you with potential partners based on location and needs
  3. We share contact information (with your permission) via email
  4. You connect directly to arrange price, timing, and payment
Are there any fees or commissions?

No! ParknStore is completely free for both homeowners and drivers. We're building a community and validating demand. You keep 100% of what you agree on.

How are payments handled?

You arrange payments directly. This could be cash on the day, bank transfer, or any method you both agree on. We don't handle payments - we just make the introduction.

What happens after I submit my details?

We'll email you within 24 hours with contact information for potential matches. Then it's over to you to connect and make arrangements directly.

Who is responsible for the parking agreement?

The parking agreement is solely between the homeowner and the driver. ParknStore is not party to any agreements and does not guarantee the condition, safety, or availability of parking spaces. We recommend that both parties:

  • Discuss and agree on terms clearly before the event
  • Confirm pricing, timing, and any specific requirements
  • Exchange contact information and keep each other informed
  • Resolve any issues directly with each other
