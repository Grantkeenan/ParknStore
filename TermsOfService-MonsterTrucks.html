Connection Service Model

Last Updated: October 03, 2025

Important Notice: ParknStore operates as a connection service only. We connect space owners with potential renters but do not process payments, facilitate bookings, or charge any fees during this phase.

1. Nature of Our Service

ParknStore is a connection platform that connects space owners ("Hosts") with individuals seeking parking or storage solutions ("Seekers"). We provide a directory service where:

  • For Seekers: Browse listings and contact Hosts directly with no fees
  • For Hosts: List your space for free and connect with potential renters
  • Direct Arrangements: All rental agreements, payments, and arrangements are made directly between Hosts and Seekers

2. No Fees During This Phase

During our current service phase:

  • Free Listings: Hosts can list their spaces at no cost
  • Free Connections: No fees for connecting with potential renters
  • No Success Fees: We don't charge commissions on rental income
  • Free for Seekers: Seekers can browse and contact Hosts without any fees

3. Platform as Connector Only

ParknStore serves as an introduction service only. We are not involved in:

  • Payment processing between parties
  • Rental agreement creation or enforcement
  • Dispute resolution between Hosts and Seekers
  • Insurance or liability coverage
  • Refunds or financial transactions

4. User-to-User Transactions

All financial transactions, rental agreements, and arrangements are solely between Hosts and Seekers. ParknStore recommends that both parties:

  • Create written rental agreements
  • Verify each other's identities
  • Maintain appropriate insurance coverage
  • Follow local regulations and bylaws

Hot Wheel Event

Connection Service Phase: ParknStore is currently operating as a connection service only. During this period, including the Hot Wheel event, we facilitate connections between space owners and renters without any fees.

Current Service Model

During our connection service phase:

  • No Fees: Hosts receive connections at no cost
  • Direct Communication: Users communicate and arrange rentals directly
  • No Payment Processing: We do not handle any financial transactions
  • User-Managed Arrangements: All rental terms and payments are managed between users

Service Limitations

As a connection service only:

  • No Refunds: We do not process payments, therefore we do not offer refunds
  • No Dispute Resolution: Any issues between users must be resolved directly
  • No Guarantees: We do not guarantee the quality, safety, or availability of spaces
  • User Responsibility: Users are responsible for their own due diligence

Important: Your participation in the Hot Wheel event acknowledges that ParknStore serves only as a connection service. All rental arrangements, payments, and dispute resolution are handled directly between space owners and renters.

2. Definitions

  • ParknStore: The ParknStore.co.nz platform, operated as a connection service.
  • Host: A person or entity listing a parking or storage space on the ParknStore platform.
  • Seeker: A person or entity searching for parking or storage spaces through the ParknStore platform.

  • Space: The specified parking or storage area at the Host's property.
  • Listing: A Host's advertisement for their space on the ParknStore platform.

3. Platform Role

ParknStore acts exclusively as a connection service. We provide a platform for Hosts to list their spaces and for Seekers to find and contact Hosts. We are not involved in the subsequent rental arrangements between users.

Connection Service Only: ParknStore facilitates introductions but does not process payments, create rental agreements, act as an intermediary in user transactions, or charge any fees for connections during this phase.

What We Do

  • Provide a platform for Hosts to create listings
  • Allow Seekers to search for and view available spaces
  • Facilitate initial contact between Hosts and Seekers
  • Provide connection services at no cost during this phase

What We Don't Do

  • Process payments between users
  • Create or enforce rental agreements
  • Provide insurance or guarantee space availability

  • Offer refunds or handle financial transactions

4. User Responsibilities

Host Responsibilities:

  • Provide accurate and truthful descriptions of Spaces
  • Ensure Spaces are safe, legal to rent, and comply with all local regulations
  • Maintain appropriate insurance for their property
  • Respond promptly to Seeker inquiries
  • Handle all rental arrangements and payments directly with Seekers
  • Resolve any disputes directly with Seekers

Seeker Responsibilities:

  • Verify that Spaces meet their needs before contacting Hosts
  • Use Spaces only for lawful purposes
  • Maintain insurance for stored vehicles or items
  • Make all payments directly to Hosts according to mutually agreed terms
  • Conduct due diligence on Hosts and spaces before entering agreements
  • Resolve any disputes directly with Hosts

Important: ParknStore does not verify the identity, reliability, or suitability of users. All interactions and agreements between Hosts and Seekers are at their own risk.
